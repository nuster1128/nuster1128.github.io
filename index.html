Zeyu Zhang

Zeyu Zhang is currently a PhD student at Gaoling School of Artificial Intelligence, Renmin University of China under the supervision of Prof. Xu Chen in AI Engine Lab. His current research interests include memory of LLM-based agents, agent personalization, and self-evolving agents. He is currently a visiting PhD student at NExT++ at the National University of Singapore, working with Dr. Yang Zhang and Prof. Tat-Seng Chua.
